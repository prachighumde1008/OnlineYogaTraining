About Yoga AI

🌿 What is Yoga AI?

Yoga AI is an intelligent yoga training system that helps users practice yoga with real-time feedback. It uses camera-based pose detection to analyze your posture and guide you toward correct alignment.

🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is to make yoga accessible, accurate, and interactive. Whether you are a beginner or advanced practitioner, Yoga AI helps you improve safely and effectively.

✨ Features

  • ✔ Real-time pose detection using AI
  • ✔ Accuracy tracking for each pose
  • ✔ Progress monitoring with history
  • ✔ Guided practice through video tutorials
  • ✔ Easy-to-use interface

💡 Why Use Yoga AI?

Practicing yoga alone can lead to incorrect posture. Yoga AI ensures you perform each pose correctly, helping prevent injuries and improving results faster.
